Welcome to Northwest Bimmer


Northwest Bimmer is an enthusiast owned and run garage based in the North West of the uk, specialising in all models of BMW either classic or modern. We have a variety of specialist BMW specific workshop and diagnostic tools enabling us to provide a dealership level service. Established in 2015, our team comprises of former dealership techs and experienced individuals with at least 10 years experience each in the bmw automotive industry.
