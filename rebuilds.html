Engine Rebuilds

We specialise in BMW engine rebuilds and repairs. Our two engine builders have over forty years combined experience in the motor trade. The proprietor and lead technician served in apprenticeship at Mercedes Benz, followed by years of experience working with all German brands. Rest assured your car will be in safe hands. A large proportion of our workshop time is focused on BMW engine rebuilds. This is driven by our awareness of customer demand & our proven expertise in full engine rebuilds.
